Mahasehat

Project Leader and Developer

Mahasehat is a healthy lifestyle app designed for university students. Created using Microsoft Power Apps and Sharepoint for user database management, it features healthy lifestyle education content, step tracking, sleep tracking, healthy lifestyle planning, and a BMI calculator. The app provides information and tips on healthy living, helps users monitor their daily physical activity by counting steps, allows them to record and track their sleep patterns, assists in planning and following a healthy routine, and enables users to calculate their Body Mass Index and receive recommendations based on the results. Mahasehat aims to support students in adopting a healthier, more balanced lifestyle.
